Orthopedic

Our Orthopedic department offers comprehensive diagnostic, treatment, and rehabilitation services for a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions, ensuring optimal mobility and recovery

Led by a team of experienced orthopedic surgeons and specialized healthcare professionals, we offer a wide range of diagnostic, treatment, and rehabilitation services tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient.

Radhe Krishna Hospital provides exceptional orthopedic care through a dedicated team, which includes:

  • Orthopedic Surgeons
  • Sports Medicine Specialists
  • Orthopedic Anesthesiologists
  • Physical Therapists
  • Rehabilitation Specialists

Orthopedical Solutions

  • Our Orthopedic Services cover a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions, including:

      Joints/Bone/Muscle pain Joint effusion(Water/Pus/Bloodcollection in joint space) Chronic backache Arthritis

  • Our Orthopedic department utilizes advanced diagnostic techniques and state-of-the-art imaging technologies to accurately diagnose musculoskeletal conditions, including:

      Synovial fluids examination Blood/Urine reports X-ray Ultra sound

  • Once a diagnosis is made, our team works closely with patients to develop personalized treatment plans tailored to their individual needs. Treatment options may include:

      Medication Physiotherapy Rehabilitation Synovial fluid taping
