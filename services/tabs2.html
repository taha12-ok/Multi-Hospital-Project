Our Orthopedic Services cover a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions, including:

  • Joints/Bone/Muscle pain
  • Joint effusion (Water/Pus/Blood collection in joint space)
  • Chronic backache
  • Arthritis

Our Orthopedic department utilizes advanced diagnostic techniques and state-of-the-art imaging technologies:

  • Synovial fluids examination
  • Blood/Urine reports
  • X-ray
  • Ultrasound

Once a diagnosis is made, personalized treatment plans may include:

  • Medication
  • Physiotherapy
  • Rehabilitation
  • Synovial fluid taping
